Project

Little Potions Prepper (LPP) is a miniaturized-perspective “cooking” game where you play as the 6-inch tall assistant of a busy witch. As you spawn within her potions room, you quickly realize the world was not built for someone your size. Using brawn and magic, you’ll need to craft and implement methods of accessing and transporting various ingredients around the room.
